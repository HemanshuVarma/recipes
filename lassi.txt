Ingredients :
 - 750 gm yoghurt
 - 50 gm ice cubes
 - Salt or sugar
 - 200 ml water
Directions :
 1. Crush the ice cubes in a blender.
 2. Add the yoghurt, water, salt or sugar.
 3. Blend for about 1 minute.
 4. Serve chilled.
 5. If you are making a namkeen lassi you can add masala like bhuna jeera and kala namak and as desired.
